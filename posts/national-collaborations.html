Tackling Teacher Shortages
I contributed two stories to this national collaboration on teacher shortages: one, with the Associated Press, that examined hiring patterns and vacancies, as well as a feature on an Alabama school district trying to bridge Spanish-speaking staffing gaps. My coverage of English learners was highlighted by Education Writers Association judges, who recognized the package as a finalist for collaborations at the organization's national seminar.
