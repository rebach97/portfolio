About Me

I am an Alabama-based journalist specializing in investigative reporting, public records, and data-driven storytelling. My reporting focuses on education equity, policy, and systemic challenges impacting schools and communities across the South.

I currently serve as AL.com's education and data reporter, leading the education team's K-12 coverage and assisting journalists across the newsroom with data analysis and visualization. I joined AL.com in 2021 as a Report for America corps member, where I was a founding member of the outlet's Education Lab, focusing on opportunity gaps and equity issues in Alabama schools. My prior journalism experience includes a yearlong stint on the New York Times' Pulitzer-winning COVID-19 Tracking Project...
